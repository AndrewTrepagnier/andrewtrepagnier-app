Intro.

Hey internet people, my name is Andrew Trepagnier and this is my website were you can learn more about me, stay updated on what I'm working on, or read my blog.

I usually write about my thoughts on new tech, my experiences, start-ups, and alot of other really random, niche things.

What is this? Why?

I built this website using Django, the same python-based web framework that famous platforms like Instagram, Pintrest, Quora, and many more first used in development.

There are a couple reasons why I wanted to make my own website - the first being I really wanted to learn web development, so this was the perfect personal project to start with.

Any CS major would probably cringe at the backend of this site, but thats okay, every project of mine starts with a barely functional, horrendous proof of concept.

The second reason - Document Control. I use this as a more permanent archive of my thoughts, blog posts, and ideas that is custom curated to my liking.

I won't lose any of my data this way. It will stay live for as long as I live and act as a sanctuary for my stuff without the need for any annoying ads, aquisitions, or data breaches that get in the way of good writing.

I also like keeping it very minimalistic and dead simple.

Okay, What next?

Take a look around the website! I am constantly developing, iterating, and writing.
